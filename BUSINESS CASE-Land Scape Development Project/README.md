# Landscape Development for Environmental Risk Mitigation — Business Case

## 📌 Objective
The objective of this project is to develop a business case evaluating landscaping interventions to mitigate recurring flood risk, soil erosion, and land degradation. The analysis compares preventive solution options using financial and environmental appraisal to recommend the most sustainable, cost-effective investment ahead of the monsoon season.

---
## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Word
- Cost-Benefit Analysis (CBA)
- ROI (Return on Investment) Modeling
- NPV (Net Present Value) Calculation with Discounted Cash Flow (10% discount rate)
- Payback Period Analysis
- Risk Assessment Matrix
- Options Appraisal Framework
- Project Timeline Planning

---
## ✨ Features
- Executive Summary with Quantified Recommendation
- Problem Statement & Environmental Risk Context
- Root-Cause Analysis of Flooding & Erosion
- Three Solution Options Compared (Do Nothing / Basic Preventive / Integrated Flood-Prevention)
- Pros, Cons & Risk Rating per Option
- Cost-Benefit Financial Summary per Option
- Discounted NPV, ROI & Payback Period Comparison
- Property Value Impact Analysis
- Final Recommendation with Supporting Benefits
- 6-Month Phased Implementation Timeline

---
## 📊 Business Insights
- Identified that lack of vegetation and drainage infrastructure directly drives flood and erosion risk.
- Quantified annual savings: ₹1,80,000 (Basic option) vs ₹3,00,000 (Integrated option).
- Projected property value increase of ₹6,00,000 by Year 5 under the Integrated option.
- Confirmed positive NPV for both options using a 10% discount rate over 5 years.
- Demonstrated that higher upfront investment in the Integrated option delivers stronger long-term ROI and risk reduction.
- Aligned project delivery with seasonal risk (completion before monsoon).
