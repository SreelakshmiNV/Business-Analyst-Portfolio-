# Sample Superstore Excel Dashboard

## 📌 Objective

The objective of this project is to develop an interactive sales dashboard using Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance, customer distribution, product profitability, regional sales trends, and order patterns. The dashboard enables businesses to monitor key sales KPIs, identify profitable products and regions, track monthly sales and profit trends, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations and dynamic filters.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- KPI Cards
- Conditional Formatting
- Data Validation

---

## ✨ Features

- Interactive Sales Dashboard with KPI Cards
- Customer and Product Analysis
- Regional Sales Analysis
- Profit Analysis by Sub-Category
- Ship Mode Analysis
- Monthly Sales & Profit Trend Analysis
- Top 10 Profitable Orders
- Top 5 Products by Profit
- Interactive Slicers and Filters
- Dynamic Sales Reporting

---

## 📊 Business Insights

- Identified total customers, products, cities, and overall sales performance.
- Analyzed customer distribution across different cities.
- Identified the most profitable product sub-categories.
- Evaluated order distribution across different shipping modes.
- Tracked monthly sales and profit trends over time.
- Identified top-performing products based on profit.
- Analyzed regional product sales distribution.
- Highlighted the most profitable customer orders to support business strategy.
- Enabled interactive analysis using filters for Year, State, and Segment.
