# Smart-Flame: Intelligent Gas Cooking System — Business Case

## 📌 Objective
The objective of this project is to develop a business case evaluating smart, sensor-based gas stove technology to reduce LPG consumption and improve cooking safety. The analysis compares multiple solution options using financial appraisal techniques to recommend the most viable path for product development, supporting data-driven investment decision-making.

---
## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Word
- Cost-Benefit Analysis (CBA)
- ROI (Return on Investment) Modeling
- NPV (Net Present Value) Calculation
- Payback Period Analysis
- Risk Assessment Matrix
- Options Appraisal Framework

---
## ✨ Features
- Executive Summary with Quantified Recommendation
- Problem Statement & Market Context Analysis
- Current Process (As-Is) Breakdown
- Four Solution Options Compared (Do Nothing / Improved Standard / Smart Product / Brand Partnership)
- Pros, Cons & Risk Rating per Option
- Cost-Benefit Financial Summary per Option
- ROI, NPV & Payback Period Comparison
- Final Recommendation with Supporting Benefits
- Implementation Timeline (12–18 Months)

---
## 📊 Business Insights
- Identified that 65%+ of surveyed users cited fuel inefficiency as their primary concern.
- Quantified financial superiority of Smart-Flame: 55% ROI, ₹1.60 Cr NPV, 2.1-year payback.
- Compared four strategic paths and eliminated weaker options based on risk and return.
- Estimated 15–20% gas savings potential for end consumers.
- Projected 7-year product lifespan with low maintenance needs.
- Linked technological differentiation to brand positioning and market leadership.
